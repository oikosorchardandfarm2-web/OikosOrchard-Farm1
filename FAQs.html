About Oikos

Oikos Orchard & Farm is a sustainable agritourism destination located in the highlands of South Cebu, Philippines, at 300 meters above sea level. We specialize in producing organic fruits, vegetables, Philippine native tree seedlings, and raising native animals. We also offer glamping accommodations for nature lovers and adventurous travelers seeking an authentic farm experience.

We are located at Vegetable Highway, Upper Bae, Sibonga, Cebu, Philippines. Nestled at 300 meters above sea level, our farm offers breathtaking views of Bohol Island and is easily accessible from major towns in South Cebu.

Oikos Orchard & Farm was established in 2021 by Bernard Restiﬁcar and Myrma Restiﬁcar. Our mission is to be the first tourism park of fruit-bearing and Philippine native trees in the Philippines, promoting sustainable agriculture and ecotourism.

"Oikos" is an ancient Greek term that refers to three related but distinct concepts: the family, the family's property, and the house. The word is infused with great meaning, hope, and promise. Our name reflects our commitment to creating a "home away from home" for our visitors.
